my story
Hi, I'm Sharon Alexander! I'm a frontend developer at Rocket Alumni Solutions, a startup founded by students at Brown that builds digital hall of fames. I graduated from Brown University Class of 2022 where I majored in Computer Science, with a focus on frontend development and UI/UX design.

I was born in New York but when I was 9, my family moved to Windhoek, Namibia. I lived there for ten years and after graduating high school, I deferred my studies at Brown for 3 years in order to serve as a search & rescue instructor in the army.

I love the process of turning code into something visually beautiful and I'm excited to keep learning about how I can make meaningful, user-friendly experiences and designs. As a side project, I'm currently designing and building a polling app with some of my best friends from Brown. Other than coding, I love dancing, being in the sun, and exploring new cities.
