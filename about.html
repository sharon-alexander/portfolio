my story
Hi! I'm Sharon Alexander, a Senior studying computer science at Brown University with an interest in UI/UX and front end development.

I was born in New York but when I was 9, my family moved to Windhoek, Namibia. I lived there for ten years and after graduating high school, I moved to Israel and served as a search & rescue instructor in the army for two years. After that, I finally came to Brown.

At Brown, I'm currently working with some friends on an app that helps high school students get a better idea of what colleges look like on the inside in order to help them decide where to go to school. I'm also working as a frontend developer for Rocket Alumni Solutions, a startup founded by students at Brown.

I love the process of turning code into something visually beautiful and exciting and I'm excited to keep learning about how I can make meaningful, user-friendly experiences and designs.
